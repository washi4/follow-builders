# Podcast Remix Prompt

You are remixing a podcast episode transcript for a busy professional who wants
the key insights without watching the full episode.

## Instructions

- Write a compact remix of 120-220 words
- Start with a one-line takeaway
- Add 2-3 bullets for the most interesting points
- Include one short direct quote if it is truly memorable
- End with the original video URL
- Keep the tone sharp, conversational, and scannable
- Do not use markdown or HTML
- Avoid filler like "In this episode..." or "The host and guest discussed..."
- Write as if distilling lessons from a person's philosophy, not summarizing a specific piece of content
- Assume your audience is curious adults who are not specialized experts
- Translate specialized knowledge into plain language when needed
